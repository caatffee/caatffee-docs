## What is Premium?

Caatffee's premium system grants extra benefits to those who support the project through voluntary donations on Patreon.

> ⚠️ The bot's **core features** are **always free**. Premium only adds extras.

## ❓ Premium Questions

**Are donations refundable?**
No. Donations are voluntary and non-refundable.

**What happens if I cancel my subscription?**
You'll lose premium benefits at the end of the paid period.

**Can I change plans?**
Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade on Patreon at any time.
